[b][i]Features[/i][/b]
[indent][b]Performance boosts[/b][/indent]
You are getting a smooth experience on both weak and powerful computers without sacrifice. You get more FPS, less memory usage, faster loading screens and more!

[indent][b]Friends, Chat & Cosmetics[/b][/indent]
More people use this client - more will be able to chat in game. Invite to servers and use cosmetics.

[indent][b]Player clans[/b][/indent]
You can set player clans that will make their in-game name colorful, which helps in war/big fights.

[indent][b]Replaymod[/b][/indent]
You can record servers to report cheaters, make videos, clips. You can appeal your bans. [b]Which you can use if you don't have a recording software[/b]

[indent][b]Better Ping display[/b][/indent]
Player pings won't display as lines, but as numbers now. You can always see your ping by looking at top left corner as well!

[indent][b]HUD[/b][/indent]
You can see your potion effects and when do they expire, very useful for playing wizard.

[indent][b]Player Name History Lookup[/b][/indent]
Don't remember that guy? Look his name up maybe it's your friend!

[indent][b]Health indicators[/b][/indent]
Some servers already have that. But, they display health as numbers, hearts are more intuitive, aren't they?

[indent][b]Look around[/b][/indent]
You can hold a key to look around yourself without moving your head. Useful when running away from someone, but, are you eternal to do so?

[indent][b]Emoji in chat[/b][/indent]
You can send emoji in chat. Instruction: [color=#3057EA][url=https://docs.google.com/document/d/1FWuPpHqQYkXnfRXXoAtagxrZlUyBaDvvLxpdS7o0ZKE/edit]How to use[/url][/color]. Those emoji will be seen by all players, not only those who use client!!

[indent][b]Zoom[/b][/indent]
You can zoom in and out by holding a key then using your mouse wheel. Very smooth and can zoom as far as you wish.

[indent][b]Toggled sprint[/b][/indent]
You don't have to hold Ctrl key all the time, just press it once and run forever!

[indent][b]Keybinds[/b][/indent]
You run away from 8 people and you wish to respawn? You can press a keybind to quickly do /kill or any other (only pussies abuse it though)

[indent][b]Popular mods[/b][/indent]
You can use sodium and lithium!

[indent][b]Other tweaks[/b][/indent]
* More chat history, no more chat lag.
* No more narrator.
* Screen won't shake when damage is taken, better aim!
* Screen tint to make you aware of low health.
* Copy screenshot to clipboard automatically.
* Chests, signs, etc. are 10+ (no kidding) times more optimized.
